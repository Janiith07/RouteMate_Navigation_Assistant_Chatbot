========================================================
  PEOPLE'S HEALTH CARE — SYSTEM NAVIGATION GUIDE
  Hospital Management System — Complete User Manual
========================================================

This guide covers every page, feature, and workflow in the
People's Health Care system for all user roles.

--------------------------------------------------------
  GENERAL — ALL USERS
--------------------------------------------------------

LANDING PAGE  (/)
- Public landing page visible to anyone without login.
- Shows hospital information, doctor profile, and features.
- Two buttons: "Login"  |   "Register Now"

PATIENT REGISTRATION
- Only PATIENTS can self-register.
- Fill in: Full Name, Email, Password, Gender, Date of Birth, Phone.
- After registration, you are automatically redirected
  Login page  you can type username and password after that click sign in button

LOGIN  (/login)
- All users (patients and staff) log in here with email and password.

LOGOUT
- Click the Logout button in the bottom-left of the sidebar on
  any dashboard page.
- You are redirected to login.

COMPLETE PROFILE  (/complete-profile)
- First-time patients may be prompted to complete their profile
  before accessing all features.

── PATIENT DASHBOARD  (/patient/dashboard) ─────────────

The main overview page after login. Shows:
- Welcome banner with your name and quick links.
- Stat cards: Upcoming Appointments, Active Prescriptions,
  Lab Reports Ready, Pending Bill.
- Quick action buttons: "Book Appointment", "Edit Profile".
- Recent appointment summary.
- Profile details card (Blood Group, Age, Conditions, Allergies).


── MY APPOINTMENTS  (/patient/appointments) ─────────────

VIEWING APPOINTMENTS:
- All your appointments are listed with:
    Channeling number, Date, Time, Type, Reason, Status.
- Status values: Confirmed, Scheduled, Completed, Cancelled.
- Use the filter tabs to view: All / Upcoming / Completed / Cancelled.

BOOKING AN APPOINTMENT:
1. Click the "Book Appointment" button (top right).
2. A booking modal opens.
3. Select appointment type (General Consultation / Follow-up / etc.).
4. Enter the reason for visit.
5. Choose a date from the calendar.
6. Choose an available time slot (greyed-out slots are already booked).
7. Review booking summary — a channeling number will be assigned
   automatically on confirmation.
8. Click "Confirm Booking".
9. Appointment appears in your list with status "Scheduled".
Note: Arrive 10 minutes before your scheduled time.

RESCHEDULING AN APPOINTMENT:
- Find the appointment and click "Reschedule".
- Choose a new date and time slot.
- Confirm — the system updates the channeling number if needed.

CANCELLING AN APPOINTMENT:
- Find the appointment and click "Cancel".
- Cancellation must be done before the consultation time.
- Status changes to "Cancelled".
- The system automatically reorders channeling numbers for
  remaining patients.


── MY PRESCRIPTIONS  (/patient/prescriptions) ───────────

- Lists all prescriptions issued by your doctor.
- Each prescription shows:
    Prescription ID (e.g. RX-2026-0001), Doctor name, Date,
    Medication list with name, dosage, and duration.
    Pharmacy status: Pending / Dispensed / Cancelled.
- Prescriptions are automatically created by the doctor and sent
  to the pharmacy — you do not need to do anything.
- Once the pharmacy dispenses the medicines, status updates to
  "Dispensed" here automatically.


── LAB RESULTS  (/patient/lab-results) ──────────────────

- Shows all lab tests requested for you by your doctor.
- Each test shows:
    Test name, Requested date, Status, and Results when available.
- Status values: Pending / In Progress / Completed.
- Once the lab technician uploads results, they appear here
  automatically — you will see the report.
- You will receive a notification about test preparation
  (e.g., fasting instructions) when a test is requested.


── BILLING & PAYMENTS  (/patient/billing) ───────────────

- View all bills generated for your visits.
- Each bill includes:
    Consultation fee, Medicine costs, Lab test fees, Total amount.
- Payment status: Paid / Unpaid.
- Payment is made in person using Cash or Card.
- The cashier updates the payment status manually after you pay.


── MY PROFILE  (/patient/profile) ───────────────────────

- View and edit your personal information:
    Name, Email, Phone, Gender, Date of Birth.
- View medical info: Blood Group, Known Allergies, Medical Conditions.
- Upload or change profile photo.
- Change password.

========================================================
  SYSTEM RULES & IMPORTANT NOTES
========================================================

ROLE SEPARATION:
- Each user can only access their own role's pages.
- Attempting to visit another role's URL redirects to your dashboard.

PRESCRIPTION FLOW (end-to-end):
  1. Doctor issues prescription during consultation.
  2. Prescription auto-appears in pharmacy queue as "Pending".
  3. Pharmacist reviews: links drugs, sets qty, checks availability.
  4. Pharmacist dispenses: stock auto-deducted, status → "Dispensed".
  5. Patient sees "Dispensed" status in My Prescriptions.
  6. Cashier generates bill including medicine costs.
  7. Patient pays in person — cashier marks as "Paid".

LAB TEST FLOW (end-to-end):
  1. Doctor requests lab test (during new prescription creation).
  2. Test appears automatically in lab queue as "Pending".
  3. Lab technician processes test → updates to "In Progress".
  4. Lab technician uploads results → status becomes "Completed".
  5. Doctor views results in Lab Requests page.
  6. Patient views results in Lab Results page.

CHANNELING NUMBERS:
- Assigned automatically when an appointment is booked.
- Sequential per day (001, 002, 003...).
- If an earlier appointment is cancelled, numbers for remaining
  patients are adjusted automatically.

STOCK MANAGEMENT:
- Stock is automatically deducted when pharmacy dispenses medicines.
- Low stock alert triggers when stockQty ≤ reorderLevel.
- Out of stock alert triggers when stockQty = 0.
- Pharmacy dashboard bell icon shows live stock alerts.

MEDICAL ADVICE DISCLAIMER:
- This system does NOT provide medical diagnosis or treatment advice.
- The system helps navigate the hospital platform only.
- For any medical questions about symptoms, diseases, or treatments,
  please consult your doctor directly.
